The different races of Starcraft

Terran is a race of humans. They seek to spread their colonies across the stars while maintaining law, justice and order. Though plagued by controversy and corruption, there are a number of Terran that seek the truth and the light.

The Protoss are a hyper-advanced alien race that are the keepers and guardians of the galaxy. They are vehement enemies of the Zerg who seek to overwhelm all life.
